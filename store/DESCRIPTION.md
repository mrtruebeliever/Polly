# Pebble app store listing

**Title:** Polly

**Tagline:** Your talking parrot.

**Description:**

Polly is a colourful parrot for your Pebble Time 2 that actually talks out loud
through the watch speaker.

- **SELECT** — dictate a sentence and Polly speaks it back.
- **UP** — ask a question and Polly answers in a sentence or two, powered by
  Google Gemini's free tier.
- **DOWN** — pick one of your own preset "quick phrases" and say it instantly.

Speech is generated by Google Cloud Text-to-Speech, with dozens of languages and
voices to choose from. Between chats the parrot blinks, tilts its head, steps,
hops and flaps on its branch.

Requires a Pebble Time 2 (speaker + microphone). Bring your own free Google API
keys — Cloud Text-to-Speech for the voice, and an AI Studio (Gemini) key for the
optional ask-AI button. Both are entered in the app settings and stay on your
phone. Voice, language, volume, animation frequency and quick phrases are all
configurable.

## Release notes — 1.3.0

- Polly can now speak much longer sentences — long phrases that used to fail with
  "Audio too large" or get cut short now play in full.
- Dictated sentences and preset quick phrases are no longer truncated
  mid-sentence (now up to 200 characters).
- More reliable speech: the TTS audio is streamed to the speaker as it arrives
  instead of being buffered all at once.

## Release notes — 1.2.0

- On-watch button-hint icons (ask / dictate / quick phrases).
- BACK now returns to the home screen from an error or busy state instead of
  exiting the app.
